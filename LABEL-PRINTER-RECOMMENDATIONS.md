# Label Printer Recommendations for InterShip

## Thermal Label Printers (4x6 inch)

These are the most common size for shipping labels and work great with our print functionality.

### 1. **Brother QL-1100**
- **Price Range**: $150-200
- **Label Size**: 4" x 6" (and other sizes)
- **Type**: Direct Thermal
- **Pros**: 
  - Fast printing
  - Good for medium volume
  - Works well with standard printers
- **Cons**: Labels fade over time (direct thermal)
- **Best for**: Office environments, moderate shipping volume

### 2. **Zebra ZD220**
- **Price Range**: $200-300
- **Label Size**: 4" x 6"
- **Type**: Direct Thermal
- **Pros**:
  - Industrial quality
  - Very reliable
  - Fast printing
  - Good for high volume
- **Cons**: More expensive
- **Best for**: High-volume shipping operations

### 3. **Dymo LabelWriter 450**
- **Price Range**: $100-150
- **Label Size**: 4" x 6" (plus other sizes)
- **Type**: Direct Thermal
- **Pros**:
  - Affordable
  - Easy setup
  - Good for small businesses
- **Cons**: Lower volume capacity
- **Best for**: Small offices, low to medium volume

### 4. **Rollo Label Printer**
- **Price Range**: $170-200
- **Label Size**: 4" x 6"
- **Type**: Direct Thermal
- **Pros**:
  - Works with any thermal labels
  - Fast printing
  - Good value
  - Compatible with many platforms
- **Cons**: Limited brand recognition
- **Best for**: Small to medium businesses

## Recommendations by Use Case

### **For Shipping Labels (Recommended)**
1. **Best Value**: Dymo LabelWriter 450 or Rollo Label Printer
2. **Best Quality**: Zebra ZD220
3. **Best Overall**: Brother QL-1100

### **For Internal Package Labeling**
- Brother PT-D600 or Phomemo M110 for small labels
- Any 4x6 printer for standard labels

## Label Size Recommendations

Our print layout is optimized for **4" x 6" (4x6 inch)** labels, which is the standard shipping label size. When purchasing:

- **Shipping Labels**: 4" x 6" direct thermal labels
- **Internal Labels**: Can use smaller sizes (2" x 4", 3" x 5")
- **QR Code**: Works best at 200x200px or larger (our default)

## Setup Tips

1. **Print Settings**: In browser print dialog, select your label printer
2. **Page Size**: Set to 4" x 6" if available
3. **Margins**: Set to minimum (0.25" or less)
4. **Scale**: 100% (no scaling)

## Amazon Search Terms

- "4x6 thermal label printer"

## Notes

- Direct thermal labels don't require ink/toner
- Labels may fade over time (6-12 months)
- For long-term storage, consider thermal transfer printers (more expensive)
- Our print layout automatically formats for 4x6 labels
